Bodybuilding Workouts

Here are some effective bodybuilding workouts to help you build muscle:

Legs Workout

A workout focused on strengthening and toning your leg muscles.

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press
  • Deadlifts

Chest Workout

A workout targeting your chest muscles for size and definition.

  • Bench Press
  • Push-ups
  • Dumbbell Flyes
  • Chest Dips

Shoulders Workout

A workout to develop strong and sculpted shoulder muscles.

  • Overhead Press
  • Side Lateral Raises
  • Front Raises
  • Upright Rows

Biceps Workout

A workout to target your biceps for size and strength.

  • Barbell Curls
  • Dumbbell Curls
  • Hammer Curls
  • Preacher Curls

Triceps Workout

A workout to target and strengthen your triceps muscles.

  • Triceps Dips
  • Close Grip Bench Press
  • Triceps Pushdowns
  • Skull Crushers

Back Workout

A workout to develop a strong and defined back.

  • Deadlifts
  • Lat Pulldowns
  • Rows (Barbell or Dumbbell)
  • Pull-ups

Abs Workout

A workout to strengthen and tone your abdominal muscles.

  • Crunches
  • Plank
  • Leg Raises
  • Russian Twists
